The game has grown the most in states with the highest population of white people, data shows

By Erin Maher

The paddle sport of pickleball is sweeping the nation and is the largest-growing sport in America, per the 2024 Sports & Fitness Industry Association (SFIA) Topline Participation Report.

According to data from USA Pickleball, the national governing body of the sport of pickleball in the United States, there are close to 41,000 pickleball courts in the United States.

Based on the data from USA Pickleball, pickleball is the most popular in the states, where at least half the population is white, according to the U.S. Census Bureau. Maine, Utah, Vermont and Wyoming are the states that have the highest number of pickleball courts per 1,000 people, and all have at least a 75% population of white people.
